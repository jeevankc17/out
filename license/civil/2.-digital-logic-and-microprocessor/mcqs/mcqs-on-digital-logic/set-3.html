101. What is the 2’s complement of 0011 0101 1001 1100?

  1. 11001001110010111100 1001 1100 10111100100111001011
  2. 11001010011001001100 1010 0110 01001100101001100100
  3. 11001010011000111100 1010 0110 00111100101001100011
  4. 11001010111111111100 1010 1111 11111100101011111111
Show me the answer

Answer: 3. 11001010011000111100 1010 0110 00111100101001100011

Explanation:

  • To find the 2’s complement:
    1. Invert all bits: 11001010011000111100 1010 0110 00111100101001100011
    2. Add 1 to the least significant bit (LSB): 1100101001100011+1=11001010011001001100 1010 0110 0011 + 1 = 1100 1010 0110 01001100101001100011+1=1100101001100100.
